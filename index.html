About the College

Dwaraka Doss Goverdhan Doss Vaishnav College, a linguistic minority institution established in 1964, is a haven for enthusiastic learners. Founded on the principles of Vaishnavism to impart value-based quality education, the college has seen phenomenal growth in its infrastructure and a constantly revamped curriculum. Outstanding performance in academics and extension activities has enabled the college to emerge as one of the premier institutions of higher learning.

About the Department

The Department of Biotechnology (est. 2001) aims to offer quality higher education and motivate students to reach the pinnacle of success. Offering B.Sc and M.Sc programs, the department focuses on building young talent for research and various life science fields. With state-of-the-art laboratories and a dedicated library, we provide a pioneering curriculum focusing on practical training in environmental biotechnology, molecular cell biology, recombinant technology, and bioinformatics.

About the Club

TECHNOSAVIOUR, the Biotechnology Student Association, is a powerhouse of intellectual exploration where curiosity meets innovation. We are dedicated to igniting young minds and nurturing talents beyond the classroom. Through cutting-edge research exposure and stimulating co-curricular activities, we empower students to push boundaries, challenge conventions, and pioneer breakthroughs in biotechnology. At TECHNOSAVIOUR, we don’t just learn—we lead, discover, and redefine the future of science.
