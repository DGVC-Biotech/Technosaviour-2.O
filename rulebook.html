Rulebook for TECHNOSAVIOUR 2025

Welcome to the official rulebook. Please read the rules carefully before registering.

General Instructions

  • Participants are requested to bring their college ID card and/or a bonafide certificate.

  • Participants must fill the Registration link to confirm their participation before 11.09.2025.

  • Registration fee is Rs. 100/- per participant, payable via the GPay details on the home page.

  • Each participant can register for a maximum of two events. Please review the schedule below to avoid time conflicts.

  • For group events, each student must register individually.

  • Participants are requested to be present 15 minutes prior to the inaugural function.

  • Registration confirmation and spot registrations will take place between 08:00 AM and 09:15 AM on 12.09.2025. No further registrations will be accepted after this time.

  • Participants must reach their respective event venues on time. The schedule is provided below.

  • Any form of misconduct, cheating, sabotage, vulgarity, or plagiarism will lead to immediate disqualification.

  • The decisions of the judges are final and binding.

  • Refreshments and lunch will be provided to all participants.
